I’m a full-stack web and mobile developer and UI/UX designer who turns ideas into thoughtful, high-performance digital products. I care deeply about how things work, how they feel, and how they scale over time.

My work lives at the intersection of engineering and design. I build clean, maintainable systems while crafting interfaces that are intuitive, visually balanced, and user-focused — never accidental.
